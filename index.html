Procrastinating: A simple how to

Step 1

The first step to procrastinating is actually STARTING your work. Now you may be thinking, "But isn't the whole point to NOT do my work?"... To that I say, "Yes that's exactly right!" By starting your work, you open up opportunities to look for excuses to stop working.
